Who we are ?

The EVO robot ENSIT club was created in December 2011 by a group of students from the mechanical and electrical department of the National Hight School of Engineers of Tunis. It focuses on new technologies in the robotics field. It is targeted at all ENSIT students with their different specialties. EVO robot is a meeting and exchange point between students or professionals who wish to further improve their knowledge in the field of robotics and its new technologies.
